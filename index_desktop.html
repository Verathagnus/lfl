About Us

Introduction

Leofelis Instruments Pvt. Ltd. specializes in engineering prototyping and innovative instrument development across defense, aerospace, fisheries, and agriculture sectors, providing prototyping solutions to support MSMEs and foster innovation.

Vision & Mission

To become India's leader in engineering prototyping and R&D, delivering innovative solutions through strategic collaborations and advanced technologies while fostering research and skill development.

Establishment

Headquartered at Assam University campus with state-of-the-art FabLab facilities in Silchar, serving as a hub for innovation and advanced manufacturing since 2020.
