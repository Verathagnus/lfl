About Leofelis Instruments

Who We Are

Leofelis Instruments Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-thinking innovation hub specializing in engineering prototyping and cutting-edge instrument development across defense, aerospace, fisheries, and agriculture sectors.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To emerge as India's premier engineering prototyping and R&D hub.

Mission: Deliver cutting-edge solutions through strategic partnerships and advanced technologies.
